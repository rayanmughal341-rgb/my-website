Who We Are

Two Decades of
Trusted Building

Founded in 2005 in Riyadh, Najad Construction has grown into one of Saudi Arabia's most respected construction firms. We've built everything from high-rise commercial towers to luxury residential compounds and critical infrastructure.

Our team of over 800 engineers, project managers, and skilled workers brings global standards to every project — with deep roots in Saudi culture, regulation, and the unique demands of the Gulf's climate and terrain.

We are proud partners in the Kingdom's Vision 2030 transformation, delivering projects that shape communities for generations.
